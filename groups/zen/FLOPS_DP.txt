SHORT Double Precision MFLOP/s

EVENTSET
PMC0  RETIRED_INSTRUCTIONS
PMC1  CPU_CLOCKS_UNHALTED
PMC2  RETIRED_X87_FLOPS_ALL
PMC3  RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_DOUBLE_ALL

METRICS
Runtime (RDTSC) [s] time
Runtime unhalted [s]  PMC1*inverseClock
CPI   PMC1/PMC0
IPC   PMC0/PMC1
DP MFLOP/s    1.0E-06*((PMC3*2) + PMC2)/time

LONG
Formulas:
CPI = CPU_CLOCKS_UNHALTED/RETIRED_INSTRUCTIONS
IPC = RETIRED_INSTRUCTIONS/CPU_CLOCKS_UNHALTED
DP MFLOP/s = 1.0E-06*(RETIRED_SSE_AVX_FLOPS_DOUBLE_ALL*2 + RETIRED_X87_FLOPS_ALL)/time
-
Profiling group to measure double precisision FLOP rate.
